About Us Page

Welcome to Artistic Taste, your number one source for all things art related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of creative insight, with a focus on art styles, art products, and more.

Founder

Shahd Toukan

"We all have artistic creativity running through our veins, we just have to find the source it wants to go to." Shahd Toukan is a 3rd year Computer Science major in HTU. She's dedicated to all things that might inspire or get you to think outside the box. She fell in love with the arts when she was a child and has since learned to find the beauty in everything. She's a firm believer that art is not just what we paint or draw but what we feel, see, hear, and read. She loves documentaries on serial killers, the study of the psyche, and the sounds of Mongolian death metal in the morning.
